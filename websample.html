Udghosh

Udghosh is a National-level Open Quiz Competition held Annually at KIIT Bhubaneswar.It is one of the
most significant events of its kind in the country, attracting participants of all ages and backgrounds, from
school children to corporate professionals with years of quizzing experience. The event aims to provide a
platform for exposure among students and quizzing enthusiasts, connecting present-day quizzers with
legends from the past.

Our QuizMaster
